PTU contacts SPACS with all data once every 5 days
System sends out an alert if it doesn't receive data within 5 days
Will do an urgent check in if something seems out of order

Data From The PTU
  - Date/Time
  - pH (acidity)
  - ORP (chlorine level)
  - TA (total alkalinity)
  - Temperature
  - Water Hardness level
  - date/time of last filter operation
  - Water Flow Rate through filter
  - Chlorinator Status
  - Water Level Status
  - Alarms
    - Water Level Too Low
    - pumps fail to operate
  - Measurements should fall within a recommended and a safe range
    - Alert if outside recommended range
      - Action within a month
    - Warning if outside safe
      - No use until resolved
